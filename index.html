About

Why 23.4°?

The Earth sits at an axial tilt of 23.4 degrees. That's it. That one angle gives us seasons, harvests, migration patterns, weather systems — basically everything that makes life on this planet tick. One small number, absolutely massive impact.

We liked that idea. Not because we think we're as important as the literal tilt of the Earth (we're from Tameside, we're not that full of ourselves), but because we believe small, thoughtful changes can shift everything.

A better website. Smarter SEO. AI that actually does something useful. Little tilts that change the trajectory of your business. That's what we're about.

"Small moves, properly made. That's the whole idea."
